## 推荐学习路径

1. **入门理解**: [mchall/HiddenWatermark](https://github.com/mchall/HiddenWatermark) — DWT-DCT 传统水印基本思路
2. **经典深度学习方案**: [cuihaoleo/cnn_watermark](https://github.com/cuihaoleo/cnn_watermark) 或 [yu4u/dnn-watermark](https://github.com/yu4u/dnn-watermark) — 理解 encoder-noise layer-decoder 架构
3. **进阶**: [PIMoG](https://github.com/FangHanNUS/PIMoG-An-Effective-Screen-shooting-Noise-Layer-Simulation-for-Deep-Learning-Based-Watermarking-Netw) (ACM MM 2022) 或 [DWSF](https://github.com/bytedance/DWSF) (字节跳动) — 看如何对抗各种攻击
4. **音频方向**: [wavmark](https://github.com/wavmark/wavmark) — 音频水印领域最好的开源实现
5. **前沿**: [Hidden-in-the-Noise](https://github.com/Kasraarabi/Hidden-in-the-Noise) (ICLR 2025) — 最新扩散模型+水印结合方向
